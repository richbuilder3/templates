About Benjii

Benjii started as Opsolutions, a way to assist minority business owners in their efforts to sustain and scale economic growth. In an unrelated endeavor, we decided to create The Tax Chief, a safe and secure online method of tax preparation, with the intent of providing clients access to certified public accountants (CPAs) and registered tax professionals at a fraction of the cost of traditional firms. We assumed that our targeted demographic would include individuals but learned that minority business owners were the ones who expressed the most interest and excitement in our product. Having the same customers for both of our services gave us insight into the core issues these businesses faced which included lack of financial education and financial tools. We took this insight and decided to combine Opsolutions and The Tax Chief to build an online platform that allows users to work with CPAs regardless of location and to help minority business owners accomplish their goal of gaining sound financial controls to scale their business.

What are our beliefs?

Benjii believes in the power of underserved communities. We know that providing a solution to a neglected community’s agonizing problem resonates with them because they have been craving to be heard for so long. We see they ecstatically love and support our products and services because we alleviate frustration and improve their lives.




What we are building?

Our suite of products and services includes a desktop platform that allows access to financial services that help them grow and sustain their business. Currently, we are working on providing financial statements that are created by licensed CPAs and tax return preparation that are reviewd and revised by licensed CPAs. Our product road map includes providing ways to educate minority business owners about what financial metrics are important when analyzing their business, creating a mobile application, and utilizing banks/lending institutions finance requirements to develop assessment tools.
